Privacy Policy

At Nepal Al Travel, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our services.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you make inquiries or bookings, we collect details such as your name, email address and phone number.
  • Travel Preferences: Information about your travel interests, special requirements, and past travels to enhance your experience.
  • Technical Data: We automatically collect IP addresses, browser types, and usage patterns through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communications: Records of your interactions with our customer service team.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide and improve our services:

  • Process your travel experience and send confirmations
  • Personalize your experience with tailored recommendations
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website functionality and service offerings
  • Send important updates about your travel arrangements (when applicable)
  • Analyze trends to enhance our marketing strategies

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Secure servers and encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits of our systems
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
