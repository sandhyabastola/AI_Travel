Privacy Policy

At Nepal Al Travel, we protect your personal information while delivering personalized travel planning through our AI-powered platform. This policy explains how we handle data for your account, itinerary builder, chatbot, and other services.

Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Name, email, and password for account creation
  • Profile details like travel preferences and special requirements

Travel Planning Data:

  • Destinations, dates, and activities saved in your itinerary builder
  • Chatbot conversation history to improve recommendations
  • Map interaction data from Google Maps API

Technical Information:

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Cookies and usage data (see our Cookie Policy)

How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To maintain your user profile and secure login
  • AI Services: To power our NLP chatbot and personalize responses
  • Recommendation Engine: To suggest destinations and activities based on your preferences
  • Itinerary Builder: To save and generate your travel plans
  • Map Features: To display locations and routes using Google Maps
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and enhance our services

Data Sharing

We only share data when necessary:

  • Google Maps API: Location data for map functionality
  • Payment Processors: If you make bookings through our platform (in future implementations)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law enforcement

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Chatbot Data Handling

  • Chat conversations are stored to improve the NLP model
  • You may request deletion of specific chat histories
  • Messages are analyzed only for service improvement

Itinerary Data

  • Your saved travel plans are stored securely
  • Anonymous itinerary patterns may be used for trend analysis
  • You can export or delete itineraries anytime

Security Measures

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits of our systems
  • Limited employee access to user data

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your account information
  • Download your itinerary data
  • Request deletion of chatbot conversation history
  • Opt-out of data processing (may limit functionality)
  • Disable cookies through browser settings

Contact us at privacy@nepalaltravel.com to exercise these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of significant changes through your account email or website notices.
