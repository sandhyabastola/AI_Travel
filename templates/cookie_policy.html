Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Nepal Al Travel uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our travel planning platform.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Keeping you signed in to your account
  • Remembering your travel preferences and settings
  • Analyzing how users interact with our services
  • Improving our recommendation engine
  • Personalizing your itinerary suggestions
  • Making our interactive map function properly

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Account authentication, session management Session
Preference Remember travel choices, language settings 30 days
Analytics Improve services by tracking usage patterns 1 year
Functional Enable chatbot conversations and map features 7 days

Third-Party Cookies

We use these services that may set cookies:

  • Google Maps: For interactive map functionality
  • Google Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site
  • Chatbot Services: To maintain conversation context

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (usually found in Privacy or Security sections)
  • Our cookie consent banner when you first visit the site
  • Third-party tools like the Digital Advertising Alliance

Note: Disabling cookies may affect functionality of our itinerary builder, chatbot, and map features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. Please check back periodically.
